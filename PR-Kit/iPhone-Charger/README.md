# PR Kit — iPhone Charger Cluster

**Date assembled:** 29 April 2026
**Cluster:** "best iPhone charger" — 90,500 monthly searches
**Strategic objective:** Get Rolling Square products included in the editorial articles that AI engines (ChatGPT, Perplexity, Google AI Overview, Gemini) cite when answering iPhone charger queries.
**Why this matters:** 54.5 % of all AI citations in this cluster come from editorial sources. Rolling Square currently has 0 % visibility in those answers. Editorial inclusion is the highest-leverage GEO move for this category — far higher impact than producing more on-site content.

---

## How to use this kit

The kit is structured for **one PR/marketing person to send two outreach emails** in under an hour, with all supporting assets pre-built. Workflow:

1. **Read** `01-brand-fact-sheet.md` and `02-supertiny-spec-sheet.md` end-to-end. These are the source of truth for every claim in the kit.
2. **Convert** `01`, `02`, and `03` to PDF (any markdown→PDF tool — Pandoc, Marked, or just print-to-PDF from your browser).
3. **Pull** the `assets/supertiny-size-comparison.jpg` for inline use in emails or as an attachment.
4. **Personalize** `05-email-techradar-padoan.md` and `06-email-macworld-jary.md` against the live bylines on the target articles. Send.
5. **Track** outcomes against the metrics defined in this README.

## Editorial tone & non-negotiables

- ✅ **No paid placements.** This kit is designed for earned editorial only.
- ✅ **No embargo / no strings.** Review units go out unconditionally — editors decide whether to publish.
- ✅ **Lead with measurable claims**, never vague superlatives.
- ✅ **Always offer the comparison data.** Editors cite what they can verify; cite what they can paste.
- ❌ **Don't pitch a discount code or affiliate offer.** This is editorial outreach, not media buying.
- ❌ **Don't oversell awards.** Mention CES + Red Dot once. Twice is fine. Three times is suspicious.

---

## After the first response

When an editor replies positively:

1. **Within 24 h:** ship review units (Supertiny + TAU 2 if applicable). Tracking number to the editor + PR team.
2. **Within 48 h:** send the press kit PDFs + comparison infographic + a 2-line "ready quote" from a co-founder.
3. **Within 1 week:** if they request additional data (charging speed tests on a specific device, thermal data, etc.), respond with the test results in a structured format.
4. **Post-publication:** thank-you email + share the article on Rolling Square's social channels with `@` mention. Builds the next pitch.

## When to refresh this kit

- **Quarterly** — update test data, add new awards, update price points, refresh competitor specs.
- **On product launch** — if Rolling Square ships a new charger, add it as a Pattern 6 in `04-brand-positioning-ai-optimized.md`.
- **On Apple product release** — when iPhone 18 launches (or any new MacBook/iPad), update `02-supertiny-spec-sheet.md` with verified compatibility data and re-pitch.
- **On competitor launch** — if Anker, UGREEN, or Belkin release a new sub-65 W class product, update `03-comparison-table.md`.
