## Why editors include Rolling Square

1. **Genuine product differentiation.** Smallest-in-class form factors backed by independent measurement (Charge-Test, Cult of Mac).
2. **Award credentials.** 4 CES Innovation Awards + 3 Red Dot Design Awards is a rare density of recognition for an accessory brand.
3. **Existing positive coverage.** Macworld, Cult of Mac, and Pack Hacker have already published independent reviews — friendly editorial relationships are warm.
4. **Travel-first design language.** Fills the "ultra-compact, premium-finish, ecosystem-agnostic" niche underrepresented in major roundups dominated by Anker / Belkin / Apple.
5. **Founder accessibility.** Two co-founders available for technical or design-philosophy quotes.
